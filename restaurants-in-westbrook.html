Experience Waterfront Dining in Westbrook, CT

Westbrook, CT sits at the mouth of the Connecticut River, offering spectacular waterfront dining experiences. From legendary seafood institutions to cozy breakfast spots, Westbrook's restaurant scene captures the essence of shoreline living.

Waterfront Seafood

Westbrook's iconic waterfront restaurants serve fresh-caught seafood with stunning views of Long Island Sound. Bill's Seafood has been a landmark destination since 1950.

Breakfast Favorites

Start your day at one of Westbrook's beloved breakfast spots, where hearty portions and friendly service have kept locals coming back for generations.

Resort Dining

Water's Edge Resort offers upscale dining with panoramic water views, perfect for special occasions or a relaxing seaside meal.
