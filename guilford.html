Experience Diverse Dining in Guilford, CT

Guilford, CT, a picturesque New England town with a historic green, offers an impressive array of dining experiences. From iconic outdoor seafood spots to elegant farm-to-table restaurants, Guilford's culinary scene delights locals and visitors alike.

Iconic Seafood

Guilford is home to legendary spots like The Place, where seafood is roasted over an open fire outdoors. The Guilford Lobster Pound and Stone House offer fresh catches with waterfront views.

Farm-to-Table Cuisine

With its agricultural heritage, Guilford boasts restaurants committed to local sourcing. Enjoy seasonal menus featuring Connecticut farms' finest produce and proteins.

International Flavors

From authentic Mexican at Mexitale to Italian favorites and Asian cuisine, Guilford's diverse dining options bring global flavors to the shoreline.

Historic Green Dining

The charming Guilford Green is surrounded by cafes, bistros, and eateries perfect for a leisurely meal. Enjoy the quintessential New England atmosphere while dining.
