Experience Local Dining in Clinton, CT

Clinton, CT may be a small shoreline town, but it punches above its weight when it comes to dining. Home to iconic seafood spots and a renowned vineyard bistro, Clinton offers memorable culinary experiences for locals and visitors alike.

Legendary Lobster

Clinton is home to some of Connecticut's most beloved lobster shacks, where you can enjoy hot butter lobster rolls right on the water with stunning marina views.

Vineyard Dining

Experience farm-to-table cuisine paired with local wines at Chamard Vineyards, one of Connecticut's premier wineries offering bistro dining in a beautiful setting.

Local Pubs & Grills

Clinton's casual eateries offer the perfect spot to grab a burger, enjoy some wings, or catch the game with friends in a welcoming atmosphere.

Quick Bites

From classic diners to Asian cuisine, Clinton has plenty of options for a quick, satisfying meal any time of day.
