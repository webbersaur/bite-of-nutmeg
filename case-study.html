The Challenge

After launching a brand new website in February 2026, Michael's Trattoria — a 30+ year Wallingford institution — needed to establish Google rankings from scratch. While they had some visibility for niche "italian restaurant" searches, the broad "restaurants wallingford ct" keyword (what most diners actually search) was the real prize. They were nowhere to be found.

One reason we saw such fast results: the domain has been active since 2007, giving it nearly two decades of authority with Google. Combined with their incredible volume of glowing reviews across Google, Yelp, and TripAdvisor, the foundation was already strong — it just needed the right SEO strategy to unlock it.
