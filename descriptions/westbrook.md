# Westbrook Restaurant Descriptions

## Bacari Social
Modern waterfront dining near Pilot's Point marina, serving seafood-focused dishes and craft cocktails in a stylish setting.

## Bill's Seafood
A Westbrook institution since 1950, Bill's serves lobster rolls, fried clams, and seafood platters with outdoor seating overlooking the Patchogue River.

## Bistro Mediterranean & Tapas Bar
Mediterranean small plates and entrees bringing tapas-style dining to the Westbrook shoreline.

## Charter Oak Tavern
Pub-style American dining with a full bar, offering a relaxed atmosphere along Route 1 in Westbrook.

## Coastline Brewing
Westbrook's craft brewery on Route 1, serving locally brewed beers in a taproom setting.

## Cristy's
Classic American fare in a casual, no-frills setting that's been a reliable Westbrook staple.

## Dairy Queen
National fast-food chain location on Route 1, serving ice cream treats, burgers, and chicken strips.

## Dunkin'
Quick-service coffee and breakfast chain with a convenient Westbrook location for morning commuters and beachgoers.

## Edd's Place
A beloved breakfast and lunch counter on Route 1 that's been serving Westbrook locals for years.

## El Rincon Del Sabor
Latin American restaurant bringing authentic flavors and traditional dishes to the Westbrook dining scene.

## Hartford Giant Grinder
Oversized grinders and deli sandwiches served up in generous portions, perfect for a quick and filling lunch.

## Haywire Burger Bar
Creative burgers and shakes in a fun, casual atmosphere -- a Westbrook destination for burger lovers.

## Highliner III
Neighborhood bar and grill with a full bar, offering pub fare and a laid-back vibe.

## Hog Wild Smokin' Cue
Westbrook's go-to for authentic smoked barbecue, from slow-cooked brisket to pulled pork and classic BBQ sides.

## Lenny & Joe's Fish Tale
A beloved shoreline seafood spot on Route 1, known for its iconic carousel and generous fried seafood portions.

## Leon's Pizza
Local pizza shop on Route 1 serving pies, grinders, and Italian-American favorites to the Westbrook community.

## Sammy's Restaurant
Italian cuisine in Westbrook, offering pasta, entrees, and traditional Italian-American dishes.

## Scoops & Slices
A dual-concept spot serving pizza and ice cream -- a convenient stop for a quick slice or a sweet treat.

## Shayna B's By The Sea
A rare plant-based dining option on the Connecticut shoreline, offering vegan dishes in a seaside Westbrook setting.

## Subway
National sandwich chain with a Westbrook location for quick, build-your-own subs and wraps.

## Taqueria Cinco
Mexican restaurant bringing tacos, burritos, and south-of-the-border flavors to Westbrook's Route 1 corridor.

## The Deck
Bar and grill with a casual, open-air feel near the Westbrook waterfront.

## Turtle Cafe
A morning favorite in Westbrook, serving breakfast and American comfort food in a cozy cafe setting.

## Water's Edge Sunset Grill
Resort dining at Water's Edge with panoramic Long Island Sound views, popular for special occasions, Sunday brunch, and summer sunset dinners.

## Westbrook Pizza
Neighborhood pizzeria serving classic pies and Italian-American staples to Westbrook residents.
