Experience Diverse Dining in Branford, CT

Branford, CT is one of the Connecticut shoreline's top dining destinations, with 88+ restaurants spread across the town center, waterfront neighborhoods, and Route 1 corridor. Whether you're exploring the shops around Branford Green, catching a boat to the Thimble Islands from Stony Creek, or heading to Indian Neck for a sunset dinner, you'll find restaurants for every occasion and budget.

Waterfront Seafood & Coastal Dining

Branford's coastline is dotted with iconic seafood spots. Lenny's Indian Head Inn on Linden Avenue has served lobster rolls and fried clams to generations of shoreline families. At the Branford River marina, Dockside Seafood & Grill offers fresh catches with harbor views. In the charming Stony Creek village, Owenego Inn and The Thimbleberry provide seasonal waterfront dining steps from the Thimble Island ferry dock. Nellie's is another local favorite for coastal fare near the water.

Italian Restaurants & Pizzerias

With over a dozen Italian restaurants, Branford rivals any shoreline town for authentic Italian dining. La Luna Ristorante on Main Street is known for upscale Italian cuisine, while Rossitto's Ristorante and Doma on Main offer classic trattoria experiences near the Town Green. For pizza, local favorites include Capone's Pizza Bar, Da'Carmine Pizzeria, and Branford Pizza. P&M Fine Foods is a go-to for Italian specialty groceries and prepared foods.

Cafes, Bakeries & Breakfast Spots

Start your morning at Perkatory Coffee Roasters for locally roasted coffee, or grab fresh pastries at G Cafe Bakery near the Green. Tasty Yolk and Breakfast Connections are popular weekend brunch destinations. Common Grounds offers a cozy cafe atmosphere, and The Coffee Bean provides a quick caffeine stop along Route 1. Branford Beach Donut is a seasonal favorite near Short Beach.

International & Diverse Cuisine

Branford's food scene goes well beyond seafood and Italian. Darbar India and Kamana Indian Cuisine bring authentic Indian flavors, while SIAM Orchids serves Thai dishes and Bui Vietnamese Cuisine offers pho and banh mi. Japanese food lovers can choose from Jerry-San's Sushi, Kampai, and Takumi Sushi Ramen & Lounge. For Mexican, try Guacamole's or Jalapeño Heaven.

Pubs, Bars & Nightlife

Branford's social scene centers around spots like Tommy Sullivan's Cafe on Main Street, Archie Moore's (known for their wings), and the 1928 Cocktail Club for craft cocktails. NEBCO Branford serves New England Brewing Company beers on tap, while Pearl Wine Bar offers an upscale wine-focused experience. Lockworks Tavern and Little Pub round out the casual dining and drinks options.

Getting There & Parking

Most Branford restaurants are concentrated in three areas: the Town Green and Main Street (free street parking and town lots), the Route 1 commercial corridor (plenty of lot parking), and the waterfront neighborhoods of Indian Neck and Stony Creek (limited street parking in summer). Branford is accessible via I-95 exits 54-56, and the Branford Shore Line East train station connects to New Haven and New London.
