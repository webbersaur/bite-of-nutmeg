Experience Diverse Dining in East Haven, CT

East Haven, CT, offers a surprisingly diverse dining scene along the Connecticut shoreline. From beloved waterfront seafood spots to Mediterranean tapas and international cuisine, East Haven delivers memorable meals for every palate.

Waterfront Seafood

East Haven's Cosey Beach area is home to beloved seafood spots like The Lobster Shack and Sandpiper Restaurant, offering fresh catches with stunning waterfront views of Long Island Sound.

Mediterranean & European

Discover Mediterranean flavors at Bistro Mediterranean & Tapas Bar or European cuisine at Transilvania Restaurant and Bar, bringing international sophistication to the shoreline.

Classic American

From The Rib House's finger-licking barbecue to cozy diners and taverns, East Haven serves up hearty American comfort food that keeps locals coming back.

Pizza Paradise

With legendary spots like Frank Pepe's and numerous local pizzerias, East Haven is part of Connecticut's proud New Haven-style pizza tradition.
