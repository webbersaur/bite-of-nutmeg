Wellll, This Isn't An App?

You clicked "Buy This App" and landed on a website. Plot twist!

A Bite of Nutmeg is intentionally not an app. There's no download, no app store, no updates to install. Just open your browser and you're here — every restaurant from East Haven to Old Saybrook, right at your fingertips.

Add this website to your home screen and it's almost the same exact thing.

It's Free. Seriously.

A Bite of Nutmeg is free for everyone — diners and restaurants alike. There's no paywall, no premium tier, no "unlock more results" nonsense. Every restaurant is listed because it should be, not because someone paid for placement.

This platform is going to keep growing. More towns, more features, more ways to discover great food along the CT shoreline. And it will always be free to use.

Why a Website Beats an App

  • Full control. No app store gatekeepers. No waiting weeks for approval. When something needs updating, it goes live immediately.
  • No restrictions. Apple and Google have rules about what apps can show and how they work. A website means total creative freedom to build the best experience possible.
  • No app store fees. App stores take up to 30% of every transaction. That overhead doesn't exist here.
  • Works everywhere. iPhone, Android, tablet, laptop — if it has a browser, it works. No download required, no storage space eaten up on your phone.
  • Always up to date. No waiting for app updates. Every time you visit, you're seeing the latest version automatically.

Add to Your Home Screen

It takes about 10 seconds and works just like an app — tap the icon, and you're here. No app store needed.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  • Open abiteofnutmeg.com in Safari
  • Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up)
  • Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  • Tap "Add" — done!

Android (Chrome)

  • Open abiteofnutmeg.com in Chrome
  • Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
  • Tap "Add to Home screen"
  • Tap "Add" — done!

Chromebook / Desktop (Chrome)

  • Open abiteofnutmeg.com in Chrome
  • Click the three-dot menu in the top right
  • Click "Save and share" then "Create shortcut"
  • Check "Open as window" and click "Create"
