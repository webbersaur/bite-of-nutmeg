Just Start Typing

Type 2-3 letters of any restaurant name into the search bar above. The phone number appears instantly. Tap it to call. That's it.

One search covers 330+ restaurants across 7 towns, from East Haven to Old Saybrook. Our data comes directly from local health departments, so if a restaurant is licensed to serve food on the CT shoreline, it's here.
