Experience Historic Dining in Old Saybrook, CT

Old Saybrook, CT combines historic New England charm with a sophisticated dining scene. From upscale oyster bars to beloved Main Street pubs, this picturesque shoreline town offers culinary experiences as memorable as its scenery.

Upscale Seafood

Old Saybrook's premier seafood restaurants showcase the finest coastal cuisine, from raw bar selections to expertly prepared catches in elegant settings.

Main Street Charm

Stroll down historic Main Street and discover charming restaurants, pubs, and cafes housed in beautifully preserved buildings dating back centuries.

Resort Dining

Saybrook Point Resort offers waterfront fine dining with stunning views where the Connecticut River meets Long Island Sound.

Artisan Pizza & Italian

From wood-fired artisan pizzas to traditional Italian trattorias, Old Saybrook serves up authentic flavors in welcoming atmospheres.
